HI-SEAS Mission VI cancelled

University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa
Contact:
Kim Binsted, (808) 398-1300
HI-SEAS Principal Investigator, University of Hawai‘i at Mānoa
Kelli Abe Trifonovitch, (808) 228-8108
UH Office of Communications
Posted: Feb 26, 2018

HI-SEAS Mission VI has been cancelled as a crew member has voluntarily withdrawn their participation in the study. Regrettably, it is not possible to perform a mission with a crew of three. Further, an investigation is ongoing into an incident which occurred on February 19, 2018, temporarily suspending the mission. After a full safety inspection of the habitat has been performed, a new call for applicants will be released to compose a new crew for another mission based on a review by the institutional review board.
